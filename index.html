The Concept

Your friendly Mexican cuisine.

Fuego offers a unique dining experience blending Mexican cuisine with a Saudi touch, focusing on quality, innovation, and authentic flavors. Founded in 2016 as a food truck, we became a registered brand in 2021, combining Mexican style with the passion of a Saudi chef. By 2024, we expanded, opening our first branch in Riyadh, inspired by Mexico's vibrant streets. "Fuego," meaning "Flames" in Spanish, reflects our fiery cooking, while our wolf logo symbolizes togetherness and shared meals. Explore our branches to experience the authentic flavors of Fuego!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your reservation policy?

We do accept bookings, but they are not mandatory. Anyone can enter without a reservation, but at the same time, if someone wants to book, we accept it to avoid waiting.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Check our menu for more details.

Are your dishes allergen-friendly?

We strive to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please inform our staff of any allergens or dietary requirements.

What are your operating hours?

We are open in Madinah from 12 PM till 1 AM and in Riyadh from 11 AM till 2 AM.
